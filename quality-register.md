## Quality activity types
- **Peer review** — informal review by a colleague before formal submission
- **Technical review** — structured review of technical design or code
- **Quality inspection** — formal review against defined acceptance criteria
- **Testing** — unit, integration, system, UAT, or performance testing
- **Audit** — independent assessment of processes or deliverables
- **Walkthrough** — team-based review of a document or design
