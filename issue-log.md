## Category definitions
- **Technical** — system, infrastructure, or integration problem
- **Resource** — people availability, skills gap, or team capacity
- **Supplier** — third-party delivery failure or dependency not met
- **Scope** — something delivered outside agreed scope or not delivered at all
- **Process** — breakdown in governance, communication, or ways of working
- **Stakeholder** — conflict, disengagement, or escalation from a stakeholder

## Priority definitions
- **Critical** — blocking delivery; immediate escalation required
- **High** — significant impact on schedule, budget, or quality; resolve within 5 days
- **Medium** — moderate impact; resolve within 2 weeks
- **Low** — minor impact; resolve at next sprint or stage gate

## Status definitions
- **Open** — active issue being worked on
- **Escalated** — raised to Project Board or senior stakeholder for decision
- **Resolved** — issue closed with confirmed resolution
- **Accepted** — issue acknowledged but accepted as a known constraint
