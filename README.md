prince2-agile-templates
A free, open-source library of PRINCE2 Agile delivery templates for project managers.
MIT licensed. Copy, adapt, and use freely — no attribution required.
Built from real programme delivery across ERP implementations, cloud infrastructure, telecoms, and AI SaaS environments.
---

---
What makes these different
Most PRINCE2 templates are written for pure waterfall delivery. Most Agile templates ignore governance entirely. These templates are built for the reality of regulated environments — where you need sprint cadence and stakeholder agility, but also stage gates, audit trails, and board-level reporting.
Every Agile template includes a PRINCE2 integration section prompting the team to consider: does anything from this sprint need escalating to the governance layer? That bridge between the two methods is where most hybrid delivery frameworks fall short.

How to use
Option 1 — Download individual templates
Click any template link above, click the Raw button, and save the file. Fill in the fields marked with square brackets and adapt the rest to your project.
Option 2 — Fork the whole repo
Click Fork at the top of this page to take your own copy of the entire library.
Option 3 — Clone locally

git clone https://github.com/TemitopeKadri/prince2-agile-templates.git
