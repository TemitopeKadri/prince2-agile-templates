## Standup Format Reminder

Each person answers three questions in under 60 seconds:
1. What did I complete yesterday?
2. What will I work on today?
3. Is anything blocking me?

**Rules:**
- No problem-solving in the standup — take it offline immediately after
- No status reporting to managers — this is for the team, by the team
- Blockers get a named owner and a follow-up time before the standup ends
