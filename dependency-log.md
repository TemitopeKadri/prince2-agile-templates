## Type definitions
- **Internal** — dependency on another team or workstream within the same organisation
- **External** — dependency on a third-party supplier, vendor, or partner
- **Technical** — dependency on a system, infrastructure component, or integration
- **Regulatory** — dependency on approval from a regulator or compliance body
- **Data** — dependency on data being available, migrated, or validated

## Status definitions
- **On track** — dependency expected to be met on time
- **At risk** — some concern about whether the dependency will be met on time
- **Blocked** — dependency is not being met and is actively blocking progress
- **Met** — dependency has been fulfilled and is no longer active
