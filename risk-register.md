## Category definitions
- **Schedule** — risk to timeline or milestone dates
- **Budget** — risk to approved budget
- **Scope** — risk to what is being delivered
- **Resource** — risk related to people, skills, or availability
- **Technical** — risk related to technology, architecture, or integration
- **Supplier** — risk related to third-party delivery or dependencies
- **Regulatory** — risk related to compliance or legal requirements
- **Adoption** — risk related to user acceptance or change management

## Status definitions
- **Open** — active risk being managed
- **Escalated** — raised to Project Board for decision
- **Closed** — risk has passed or been fully mitigated
- **Materialised** — risk has become an issue; transfer to Issue Log
