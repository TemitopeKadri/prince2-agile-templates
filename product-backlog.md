## Priority definitions (MoSCoW)
- **Must Have** — critical for this release; project fails without it
- **Should Have** — important but not critical; workaround exists
- **Could Have** — nice to have; include if time and budget allow
- **Won't Have** — explicitly out of scope for this release; revisit later

## Status definitions
- **Icebox** — captured but not yet prioritised
- **Backlog** — prioritised and ready to be refined
- **Ready** — refined, estimated, and ready to pull into a sprint
- **In Sprint** — currently being worked on
- **Done** — complete and accepted by Product Owner
