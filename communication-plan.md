## 6. Communication Principles

- **No surprises** — escalate issues before they become crises
- **Right message, right audience** — tailor content to the reader's level of interest and influence
- **Consistent cadence** — maintain regular updates even when there is little to report
- **Two-way** — create opportunities for stakeholders to raise concerns, not just receive information
- **Documented** — all significant communications logged for audit trail
