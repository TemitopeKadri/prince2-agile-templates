Category definitions
Scope — change to what is being delivered
Schedule — change to timeline or milestone dates
Budget — change to approved budget or contingency draw-down
Resource — change to named resources or team composition
Technical — change to architecture, tooling, or technical approach
Regulatory — change driven by compliance or regulatory requirement
Priority definitions
Critical — must be resolved immediately; blocking delivery
High — significant impact; resolve within 5 days
Medium — moderate impact; resolve within 2 weeks
Low — minor impact; resolve at next stage gate
Status definitions
Pending — raised, not yet reviewed
Approved — approved by decision authority
Rejected — rejected with reason recorded
Deferred — deferred to next stage or phase
Implemented — approved and actioned
