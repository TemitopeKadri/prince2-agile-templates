Definitions
Influence — how much power this stakeholder has to affect the project outcome
High — can stop, redirect, or significantly change the project
Medium — can influence decisions or slow progress
Low — limited direct power over the project
Interest — how much this stakeholder cares about the project outcome
High — directly affected by or invested in the outcome
Medium — some interest but not directly impacted day to day
Low — peripheral awareness only
Attitude — current disposition toward the project
Champion — actively supportive and advocating for the project
Supporter — broadly positive, willing to help
Neutral — no strong view either way
Sceptic — unconvinced; needs evidence before committing
Blocker — actively resistant or opposed
Engagement strategy
Manage closely — High influence, High interest. Involve in decisions, communicate frequently
Keep satisfied — High influence, Low interest. Keep informed at a high level, avoid surprises
Keep informed — Low influence, High interest. Regular updates, involve in reviews
Monitor — Low influence, Low interest. Minimal engagement, watch for changes
